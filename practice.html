Our Legal Practice Area

At the Cusumano Firm, we provide trusted legal services tailored to your needs. Our team brings experience, dedication, and a client-first mindset across every area we serve.

Real Estate Law

Whether you’re buying, selling, or leasing property, we help make real estate transactions smooth and secure. We also assist with zoning issues, disputes, and title concerns.

Insurance Law

We fight for fair treatment in insurance claims and disputes. From denied coverage to bad faith practices, we ensure your rights are fully protected.

Business Law

From starting a company to handling contracts and disputes, we support your business every step of the way. Our legal guidance helps you avoid risks and grow with confidence.

Personal Injury

If you’ve been injured in an accident, we work to get you the compensation you deserve. We handle cases involving car accidents, slips and falls, and more.

Medical Neglegence

When medical care goes wrong, we help you hold the responsible parties accountable. Our team handles cases involving misdiagnosis, surgical errors, and other medical mistakes.

Criminal Defense

Facing criminal charges is stressful — we’re here to defend your rights. We offer strong, strategic representation in both misdemeanor and felony cases.
